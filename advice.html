For Tips and Recommendations for Traveling to Alaska

Best Time to Visit Alaska

The most popular and, therefore, expensive months to travel are June-August. The months of September-April are ideal to see the Aurora.

What to Take

Prepare for cold weather by taking thermal underwear and waterproof clothing. Bring sunscreen. Believe us, the sun strong even in cold weather.

Students, Are You Ready?

How about working and exploring Alaska during your summer vacation? Don't forget to take your international student ID cards with you.

Be Prepared

Save emergency contact numbers in your phone and on a physical list. Don't forget to get Travel Insurance before you go.

Bear Cubs

There are many salmon in Alaska, which bears love. If you see baby bears, please do not try to pet or feed them. You should take bear spray and other safety precautions in case of any incident.

High Altitude

Many areas in Alaska are at high altitude. Be mindful about how this way affect you, especially if you have any existing health conditions.
