For Tips and Recommendations for Traveling to Alaska

Best Time to Alaska

The most popular and expensive months to travel are June-August. September-April are ideal months to see the Aurora.

Thing to Take

Prepare your suitcase by putting thermal underwear and waterproof clothing. Take your sunscreen. Believe us, it burns your skin in cold weather.

Students, are you ready?

Students, how about working and exploring Alaska during summer vacation? Don't forget to take your international student ID cards with you.

Emergency Preparedness

Save emergency contact numbers in your phone and on a physical list. Don't forget to get Travel Insurance
